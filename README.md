# Strategic BI & Information Management: The Soja™ Case

**Course:** Information Management and Visualization | 3rd Year, BSc in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science

## About the Project
This project revolves around **Soja™**, a simulated cloud infrastructure startup. The core objective was to apply advanced information management methodologies to solve critical business challenges, specifically scalability bottlenecks and service outages. Ultimately, the project aimed to transform raw operational data into strategic intelligence to support executive decision-making.

## Diagnosis & Business Analysis
To identify the root causes of the company's operational issues, we applied several strategic management frameworks:
* **Problem & Objective Trees:** Mapped the cause-and-effect relationship between technological infrastructure constraints and customer satisfaction drops.
* **Infomaps (Phases A & B):** Conducted a detailed mapping of information flows across key departments (Finance, Software, Infrastructure, and HR) to identify data silos and communication gaps.

## The Solution: Multidimensional Visualization
We developed a comprehensive Business Intelligence solution using **TIBCO Spotfire**, structured around three main pillars:
1. **Supply & Demand Analysis:** Visualized server loads (CPU, Memory, Disk usage) to optimize resource allocation and prevent overloads.
2. **Performance Monitoring (KPIs):** Built interactive dashboards to track critical business metrics, including customer churn rate, user retention, and overall satisfaction levels.
3. **Predictive Analytics (Forecasting):** Implemented forecasting models to anticipate future demand spikes and calculate infrastructure investment needs up to the year 2025.

## Key Results & Impact
* **Operational Optimization:** Successfully reduced service failures by identifying correlations between specific software versions and hardware setups.
* **Customer Retention:** Enabled the early detection of behavioral patterns leading to service abandonment (churn prevention).
* **Strategic Scalability:** Delivered a data-driven growth plan that minimizes investment risks and prepares the company for sustainable expansion.
